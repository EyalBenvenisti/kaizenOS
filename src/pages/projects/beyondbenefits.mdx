Beyond Benefits was an early-day startup that aimed to empower employees in a corporate environment by providing a wide range of lifestyle activities and services, seeking to build a one-stop-shop to offer concierge and well-being services based on company, team, and individual preferences.

I joined the founding team as their first design team member to build and ship an evergreen product.
